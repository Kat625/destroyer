Joules Calculator



Enter the mass of the object you want to destroy in kilograms in the first input box. Then, enter the density of the object in kilograms per cubic meter in the second input box. Click on the "Calculate Joules" button to see the amount of joules needed to destroy the object displayed below the button. The result will be in scientific notation. If you want to recalculate with new values, simply change the inputs and click the button again. Remember that this calculation assumes a head-on collision and does not take into account air resistance.
